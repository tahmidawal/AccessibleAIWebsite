Our Team

Three young entrepreneurs with a vision to democratize AI for small businesses.

Cameron Aberenthy

CEO and Co-Founder


Cameron Abernethy is the CEO and co-founder of AccessibleAI. A 2023 Davidson College alum, Cameron graduated with a degree in economics and also became a self-taught software and automation engineer. In addition to academics, Cameron lettered as a menber of the Davidson Varisty Men's Soccer Team.

The idea for AccessibleAI took root in his senior year, sparked by conversations with his friend and eventual co-founder, Tahmid Awal. Together they realized they shared a common dream: to make the benefits of AI and technology accessible to small businesses, a sector often overlooked in the tech revolution.

Prior to starting AccessibleAI, Cameron also worked for Tüv Süd in Singapore, where he developed automation systems with the companies AI teams to help improve the efficiency of the company's research and product development.

Tahmid Awal

CTO and Co-Founder


Tahmid Awal, CTO and co-founder of AccessibleAI, is set to graduate from Davidson College in 2024. As an honors student, he is majoring in both physics and computer science. During his tenure at Davidson, Tahmid has distinguished himself not only in academics but also in entrepreneurial initiatives.

Born in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Tahmid founded NuevaCamp in 2021. This pioneering education platform marks Bangladesh's first significant foray into making astrophysics and data science accessible to students. At just 20 years old, he established a unique venue for learners to delve into the essentials of data management and research.

In addition to his entrepreneurial ventures, Tahmid has amassed a wealth of experience in the tech industry. His resume includes roles at startups like DataShapes and significant tenure as a machine learning engineering intern at Ally Bank. Through these positions, he has honed his skills and contributed to the field of technology with a particular focus on leveraging AI and machine learning.
