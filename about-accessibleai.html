Who We Are


At a Glance

AccessibleAI is an AI consulting and services company based in Charlotte, North Carolina. It was founded in the spring of 2023, by two classmates from Davidson College who shared a common vision of making artificial intelligence accessible to everyone.

Our Philosophy

At AccessibleAI, we see AI as the great equalizer of our era — a tool that can bridge gaps, ignite growth, and open doors to limitless possibilities. We stand by the principle that access to cutting-edge AI shouldn't be a privilege of the few but a fundamental right for all. It is this belief that propels our mission and reflects our commitment to inclusivity and fairness in the technological realm.

Our Mission

The business landscape is evolving, with AI and automation at its heart. However, in this swift transformation, smaller enterprises often find themselves at a crossroads, vying to keep pace with larger entities. AccessibleAI's mission is to level the playing field. We empower small and mid-sized businesses by providing them with bespoke AI tools, not only enabling them to compete but to thrive against their larger competitors.

Our Approach

In a world where off-the-shelf AI solutions attempt to cater to all yet fully satisfy none, we takes a tailored approach. We recognize that every company is unique, with its own set of challenges and opportunities. That's why we don't just build AI; we craft personalized AI architectures that are built to maximize efficiency and impact for your company's specific needs.

Our Promise

To us, every client's success is a testament to our philosophy. We promise to walk beside you in your AI journey, from conception to execution to make sure that your AI strategy is not just successful but transformative. At AccessibleAI, your vision is our blueprint, and your aspirations are the metrics by which we measure our success.

Welcome to AccessibleAI — where AI is for everyone.
